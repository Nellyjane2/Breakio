Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to your common questions here's everything you
need to know to begin or deepen your meditation journey

How do I get started with meditation on this app?

Start with our Beginner's Path short, guided sessions for newcomers, No experience
needed, just a quiet moment and an open mind,You can begin right after signing up.

Can I use this even if I've never meditated before?

Yes! The app is beginner-friendly and easy to navigate, Just press
play and follow the voice guidance,We'll help you every step of the way.

What's the best time of day to meditate?

Anytime that suits you — morning for energy, night for rest,The
key is consistency, not timing, Choose what feels natural to you.

Is this app free to use?

Yes, we offer free daily meditations and tools,You can also unlock
premium content with a plan, No credit card is needed to start.

Will meditation really help with anxiety or stress?

Yes, meditation calms the mind and body,It lowers stress levels and boosts
emotional balance, Many users report feeling better after just one week.
